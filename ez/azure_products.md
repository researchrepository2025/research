# Microsoft Azure AI Platform - Product Announcements
## Date Range: August 1, 2025 - January 13, 2026

### Executive Summary
Between August 1, 2025 and January 13, 2026, Microsoft announced major expansions to its Azure AI building platform, with the most significant concentration of announcements occurring at Microsoft Ignite 2025 (November 18-21, 2025). The company introduced GPT-5 models, rebranded Azure AI Foundry to Microsoft Foundry, launched new infrastructure including Maia chips and Azure Cobalt processors, and expanded its agentic AI capabilities across Azure Copilot, GitHub Copilot, and Copilot Studio. Key themes included model diversity (adding Anthropic Claude models), agent orchestration platforms, and enterprise-grade AI governance tools.

### Key Statistics
- New models added: 20+ (including GPT-5 family, Claude family, multimodal models)
- Major platform launches: 6 (Microsoft Foundry, Azure Copilot, Foundry IQ, Fabric IQ, Agent Factory, Foundry Control Plane)
- Infrastructure updates: 4 (Maia chips, Azure Cobalt 200, NVIDIA GB200 VMs, Fairwater datacenter)
- General availability releases: 10+
- Preview releases: 15+
