# AWS AI Platform - Success Metrics
## Date Range: August 1, 2025 - January 13, 2026

### Executive Summary
AWS demonstrated accelerated growth in AI platform services during August-December 2025, with AWS overall achieving 20% YoY growth (highest in 11 quarters) reaching a $132 billion annualized run rate. AWS AI-specific revenue reached multi-billion dollar scale growing at triple-digit YoY percentages. Major success indicators include: Trainium2 becoming a multibillion-dollar business with 150% QoQ growth, over 50% of Bedrock token usage running on Trainium, Project Rainier activation with nearly 500,000 chips, tens of thousands of customers adopting Amazon Nova models, and significant enterprise customer wins including Toyota Motor North America and Cox Automotive deploying production agentic AI systems.
