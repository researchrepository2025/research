# IBM AI Platform - Product Announcements
## Date Range: August 1, 2025 - January 13, 2026

### Executive Summary
Between August 1, 2025 and January 13, 2026, IBM announced significant expansions to its watsonx AI building platform. Major developments included the acquisition of DataStax to enhance watsonx.data vector capabilities, the release of Granite 3.2, 3.3, and 4.0 model families with hybrid architectures, and the introduction of unified AI governance and security capabilities. IBM launched watsonx Orchestrate with agentic workflows and AgentOps observability, introduced watsonx.ai Model Gateway for multi-model access, and released Red Hat OpenShift AI 3.0 on IBM Power. The company emphasized enterprise AI readiness through partnerships with Groq, Anthropic, Salesforce, Oracle, and Lumen Technologies, while positioning watsonx as a comprehensive platform for building, deploying, and governing AI applications at scale.

### October 2, 2025 - IBM Granite 4.0 Models
**Event:** Product Release
**Category:** Granite Models

**Description:**
IBM released Granite 4.0, featuring a hybrid Mamba/transformer architecture across four model variants: Granite-4.0-H-Small (32B total parameters, 9B active), Granite-4.0-H-Tiny (7B total parameters, 1B active), Granite-4.0-H-Micro (3B parameters), and Granite-4.0-Micro (3B parameters conventional transformer). The models achieved ISO 42001 certification as the first open language model family to receive this distinction. The hybrid architecture combines Mamba-2 layers with transformer blocks in a 9:1 ratio, enabling linear scaling with context length and over 70% reduction in RAM compared to conventional transformers for long-context workloads.

**Source Quote:**
> "hyper-efficient, high performance hybrid models" designed to run on significantly cheaper GPUs with reduced operational costs.

**Source:** [IBM Granite 4.0: Hyper-efficient, High Performance Hybrid Models for Enterprise](https://www.ibm.com/new/announcements/ibm-granite-4-0-hyper-efficient-high-performance-hybrid-models) | Published: October 2, 2025

## Key Platform Capabilities Summary
