# Canada — AI Note-Taking / Meeting Assistant: Raw Research Facts

Segment: AI meeting transcription, note-taking, conversation intelligence
Geography filter: PRIMARY HEADQUARTERS in Canada only
Research date: 2026-03-04

---

## RESEARCH NOTE

This segment is notably thin for Canadian-headquartered companies. Only one active,
Canada-headquartered AI meeting assistant company was confirmed after exhaustive search:
Fellow.ai (Ottawa, ON). A second company, Dooly (Vancouver, BC), was Canadian-headquartered
and raised $105M, but was acquired by Chicago-based Mediafly in August 2024 and its
standalone platform was shut down June 30, 2025. Both are documented below.

Companies explicitly excluded after headquarters verification:
- Trint: Listed Toronto office, but PRIMARY HQ is London, UK (38-40 Commercial Road, London E1 1LN)
- Granola: London, UK
- Tactiq: "Tactiq HQ Pty Ltd" — Australian corporate structure
- Otter.ai: Mountain View, CA, USA
- Fireflies.ai: San Francisco, CA, USA
