# Canada — AI Presentation Generation: Raw Research

**Segment:** Horizontal — AI-Powered Slide/Presentation Creation Tools
**Geography Filter:** Primary headquarters in Canada only
**Research Date:** 2026-03-04

---

## Research Note

Extensive search across Canadian AI startup databases (ALL IN Top 100, Tracxn, Crunchbase, Seedtable, Wellfound), Product Hunt launches, and direct company searches found **one confirmed Canadian-headquartered company** in this segment. All major AI presentation tools (Gamma, Beautiful.ai, Decktopus, Tome, Presentations.AI, Chronicle, Slideoo, Slidekick) are headquartered in the United States, India, Turkey, or Australia. Canada does not appear to have a significant native cluster in this specific horizontal.
