## Notes on Scope

- **Cohere**: Primary HQ is Toronto, Canada. Confirmed Canadian-headquartered. Largest and most-funded Canadian AI platform company by significant margin.
- **integrate.ai**: Primary HQ Toronto, Canada. Confirmed Canadian-headquartered. Federated learning / privacy-preserving ML platform niche.
- **Stradigi AI**: Primary HQ Montreal, Quebec. Confirmed Canadian-headquartered. Self-service AutoML platform (Kepler).
- **Layer 6 AI**: Primary HQ Toronto, Canada. Confirmed Canadian-headquartered. Operates as TD Bank's internal AI/ML centre of excellence; not an independent commercial platform as of 2025.
- **Element AI**: Founded Montreal, acquired by ServiceNow (US) in January 2021. Excluded — no longer Canadian-headquartered independent entity.
- **DataRobot**: Headquartered Boston, USA. Excluded.
