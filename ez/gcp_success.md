# Google Cloud AI Platform - Success Metrics
## Date Range: August 1, 2025 - January 13, 2026

### Executive Summary
Google Cloud AI platform demonstrated exceptional growth during this period, with Q3 2025 revenue reaching $15.2 billion (34% YoY growth) and cloud backlog hitting $155 billion (82% YoY increase). The company closed more billion-dollar deals in the first three quarters of 2025 than in the previous two years combined, secured major contracts with Meta (~$10B) and Palo Alto Networks (~$10B), and achieved Leader status in Forrester's Q4 2025 AI Infrastructure Solutions Wave. Gemini models processed 7 billion tokens per minute via direct API use, with over 70% of existing Google Cloud customers utilizing AI products.
