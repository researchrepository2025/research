## Notes on Methodology

- **T-Systems and Orange partnerships:** Search results confirmed these companies have sovereign cloud partnerships with Google and Microsoft respectively, not AWS
- **EU Commission €180M tender:** AWS is eligible to bid but has not been confirmed as a winner as of the research cutoff
- **Revenue vs. Investment:** AWS's €7.8 billion figure represents infrastructure investment (capex) through 2040, not recognized revenue
- **Bechtle contract inclusion rationale:** Though announced December 2024, this 48-month contract represents ongoing sovereign cloud revenue throughout the Feb 2025–Jan 2026 research window and was included for completeness
