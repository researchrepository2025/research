## 1. Use Case Definition

### Core Problem Being Solved

Conversational AI platforms address the challenge of providing **instant, scalable, 24/7 support** to employees and customers while reducing operational costs. These systems automate repetitive queries, provide consistent responses, and enable human agents to focus on complex issues.

**Key Value Propositions:**
- **Cost Reduction:** Chatbots reduce customer service costs by **40-60%** for enterprises. [Lindy AI](https://www.lindy.ai/blog/chatbot-pricing)
- **Efficiency Gains:** Organizations leveraging chatbots are **2.1 times more likely** to deliver exceptional results. [Master of Code](https://masterofcode.com/blog/conversational-ai-trends)
- **Agent Productivity:** **64% of agents** with AI tools can dedicate more time to resolving complex problems. [Master of Code](https://masterofcode.com/blog/conversational-ai-trends)
- **Resolution Rate:** Advanced platforms like Moveworks can make workflows **up to 90% more efficient**. [Moveworks](https://www.moveworks.com/us/en/solutions/cost-reduction)

## 4. Build vs. Buy Analysis

### 4.1 When Enterprises Build Custom Solutions

**Reasons to Build:**
1. **Proprietary Requirements:** Unique business logic or workflows not supported by off-the-shelf solutions
2. **Data Sovereignty:** Strict regulatory requirements (HIPAA, GDPR, financial compliance) requiring on-premise deployment
3. **Deep Integration Needs:** Complex integrations with legacy systems or proprietary databases
4. **Competitive Differentiation:** Conversational AI as core product differentiator
5. **Full Ownership:** Long-term IP ownership and avoiding vendor lock-in
6. **Specialized Domain:** Industry-specific language models or highly specialized knowledge bases

**Typical Build Profiles:**
- Healthcare systems requiring HIPAA-compliant, on-premise deployment
- Financial institutions with proprietary risk models and compliance frameworks
- Tech companies building conversational AI as product feature
- Government agencies with classified data requirements

- **On-Premise/Air-Gapped:** For highly regulated industries (government, defense, healthcare)
