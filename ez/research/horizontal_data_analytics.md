## 1. USE CASE DEFINITION

### Core Problem Being Solved
Data Analytics/BI AI platforms address the challenge of making data insights accessible to non-technical business users through natural language interfaces, eliminating the need for SQL expertise or data science skills. These platforms democratize data access by enabling conversational interactions with databases and automated insight generation.

### Decision Framework

#### Build if:
- Analytics are genuinely unique and central to competitive position
- You have deep technical expertise in-house
- You can commit to long-term maintenance and innovation
- Budget allows for $3M-$12M+ over 5-7 years

#### Buy if:
- Platform meets 80%+ of requirements out of the box
- Time-to-market is critical (need insights within months, not years)
- Limited technical resources for ongoing maintenance
- Budget constraints favor predictable licensing costs

#### Hybrid if:
- Need both commodity analytics and unique capabilities
- Want to leverage platform strengths while maintaining differentiation
- Can integrate commercial platforms with custom components
- Budget allows for $500K-$3M+ over 5-7 years

**2025 Industry Consensus:** "When time-to-market, opportunity cost, and long-term scalability are factored in, the equation tilts heavily in favor of buying" [Build vs Buy Analytics](https://qrvey.com/blog/build-vs-buy-analytics/)
