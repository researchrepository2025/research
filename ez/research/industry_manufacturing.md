## Custom Build vs Buy Patterns

### Cost Analysis

**Custom Build Costs:**
- **Enterprise-Grade Implementation:** $100,000 to $500,000+ [Netguru](https://www.netguru.com/blog/build-vs-buy-ai)
- **Single Custom Agent Development:** $600,000 to $1,500,000 [MarkTechPost](https://www.marktechpost.com/2025/08/24/build-vs-buy-for-enterprise-ai-2025-a-u-s-market-decision-framework-for-vps-of-ai-product/)
- **Annual Maintenance:** 10-20% of AI budget ($8,999-$14,999 typical) [Netguru](https://www.netguru.com/blog/build-vs-buy-ai)
- **Monthly Maintenance (Enterprise):** $5,000-$20,000 [Netguru](https://www.netguru.com/blog/build-vs-buy-ai)
- **Post-Deployment Costs:** 65% of total costs materialize after deployment [Netguru](https://www.netguru.com/blog/build-vs-buy-ai)

**Off-the-Shelf Costs:**
- **Initial Subscription:** $200-$400/month typical starting point [Appinventiv](https://appinventiv.com/blog/custom-ai-vs-off-the-shelf-ai/)
- **Reality Check:** Hidden costs often multiply initial estimates

### Build vs Buy Decision Framework

**When to BUILD:**
- Capability underpins competitive advantage [MarkTechPost](https://www.marktechpost.com/2025/08/24/build-vs-buy-for-enterprise-ai-2025-a-u-s-market-decision-framework-for-vps-of-ai-product/)
- Involves sensitive U.S. regulatory data (PHI, PII, financials) [MarkTechPost](https://www.marktechpost.com/2025/08/24/build-vs-buy-for-enterprise-ai-2025-a-u-s-market-decision-framework-for-vps-of-ai-product/)
- Demands deep integration into proprietary systems [MarkTechPost](https://www.marktechpost.com/2025/08/24/build-vs-buy-for-enterprise-ai-2025-a-u-s-market-decision-framework-for-vps-of-ai-product/)
- Unique IP or sector-specific requirements

**When to BUY:**
- Use case is commoditized [MarkTechPost](https://www.marktechpost.com/2025/08/24/build-vs-buy-for-enterprise-ai-2025-a-u-s-market-decision-framework-for-vps-of-ai-product/)
- Speed-to-value determines success [MarkTechPost](https://www.marktechpost.com/2025/08/24/build-vs-buy-for-enterprise-ai-2025-a-u-s-market-decision-framework-for-vps-of-ai-product/)
- Proven solutions exist for the problem
- Limited internal AI expertise

### The Blended Approach (Most Common in 2025)

**80/20 Rule:**
- **80% Buy:** Purchased or subscription-based AI solutions [AI.Work](https://www.ai.work/blog/enterprise-ai-in-2025-an-80-20-balance-of-buy-vs-build)
- **20% Build:** Custom-built AI where deep integration or unique IP is critical [AI.Work](https://www.ai.work/blog/enterprise-ai-in-2025-an-80-20-balance-of-buy-vs-build)
