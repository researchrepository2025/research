## 1. Use Case Definition

### Core Problem Being Solved

AI-powered cybersecurity solutions address the exponential growth in cyber threats and the complexity of modern attack surfaces. The core problems include:

- **Threat Detection Speed:** Organizations face 61% of alerts being ignored that later cause breaches [5 Best AI SOC Platforms For 2025](https://stellarcyber.ai/learn/best-ai-soc-platforms/)
- **Volume Overload:** 61% of professionals report being overwhelmed by threat intelligence feeds; 59% struggle to make intelligence actionable [A summer of security: empowering cyber defenders with AI](https://blog.google/technology/safety-security/cybersecurity-updates-summer-2025/)
- **Skill Shortage:** Gartner predicts by 2030, 75% of SOC teams will experience erosion in foundational security analysis skills due to overdependence on automation and AI [SACR AI SOC Market Landscape For 2025](https://softwareanalyst.substack.com/p/sacr-ai-soc-market-landscape-for)
- **Attack Sophistication:** AI-driven phishing attacks surged 703% in 2024, ransomware incidents grew 126%, and supply chain attacks increased 62% [8 AI Cybersecurity Companies For 2025](https://www.sentinelone.com/cybersecurity-101/data-and-ai/ai-cybersecurity-companies/)
