# AI in Professional Services: 2025 Industry Research Report

**Research Date:** December 9, 2025
**Sector Focus:** Professional Services (Consulting, Accounting, Legal, Advisory)
**Sources:** All sources verified from 2025 publications

---

## Executive Summary

Professional services leads all sectors in generative AI adoption, with implementation rates increasing from 33% in 2023 to 71% in 2024 [McKinsey State of AI 2025](https://www.mckinsey.com/capabilities/quantumblack/our-insights/the-state-of-ai). The AI in accounting market alone is valued at $6.68 billion in 2025, projected to reach $37.6 billion by 2030, representing a 41% CAGR [CPA.com AI in Accounting Report](https://www.cpa.com/news/cpacom-issues-2025-ai-accounting-report).

**Critical Gap:** Despite widespread adoption, median ROI remains just 10%, with one-third of leaders reporting limited or no gains [BCG Finance AI ROI](https://www.bcg.com/publications/2025/how-finance-leaders-can-get-roi-from-ai). MIT research indicates 95% of generative AI projects fail to deliver measurable ROI [Axis Intelligence AI ROI Strategy](https://axis-intelligence.com/ai-roi-strategy-2025-ultimate-guide/).
