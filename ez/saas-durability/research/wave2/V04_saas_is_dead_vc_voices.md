# V04: "SaaS Is Dead" — VC and Investor Voices
## Enterprise SaaS Durability in the Agentic Coding Era
### Wave 2 Research | Compiled: March 2026

---

## Executive Summary

A distinct cohort of investors, fund managers, and market analysts has staked out an explicitly bearish position on traditional Software-as-a-Service in the 2025–2026 period. Their arguments cluster around three structural claims: AI agents and agentic coding tools collapse the cost of custom software development to a fraction of incumbent SaaS pricing; AI-native alternatives grow faster and serve customers better than seat-licensed platforms; and the $5 trillion "Software Industrial Complex" is systemically overpriced and unsustainable. These voices moved markets — a single viral research report in February 2026 wiped an estimated $285 billion from software market capitalization in a single session. This document catalogs the primary bearish voices, their specific evidence, investment actions, and the rebuttals they face.
