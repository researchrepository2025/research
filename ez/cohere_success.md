# Cohere AI Building Platform - Success Metrics
## Date Range: August 1, 2025 - January 13, 2026

### Executive Summary
Between August 1, 2025 and January 13, 2026, Cohere demonstrated significant growth across all key metrics. The company raised $600 million in total funding ($500M in August, $100M in September), increasing its valuation from $6.8 billion to $7 billion. Annualized revenue grew from $100 million in May 2025 to $150 million by October 2025, representing 50% growth in five months. Cohere announced major partnerships with Dell, SAP, Bell Canada, AMD, and government entities in Canada and the UK. The company achieved general availability of its North platform in August 2025, securing deployments with RBC, Dell, LG CNS, Ensemble Health Partners, and Palantir. With 85% of revenue from private deployments and over 1,000 enterprise customers, Cohere positioned itself as the leading security-first enterprise AI platform.
