# IBM AI Platform - Success Metrics
## Date Range: August 1, 2025 - January 13, 2026

### Executive Summary

IBM demonstrated significant momentum in its AI platform business during the August 1, 2025 to January 13, 2026 period. The company's AI book of business reached $9.5 billion by Q3 2025, nearly doubling from $5 billion in 2024. Software revenue grew 10% year-over-year to $7.2 billion in Q3 2025, with watsonx and AI solutions driving growth. IBM received recognition from both IDC and Forrester as a Leader in multiple AI-related categories. The company raised its FY 2025 revenue growth outlook to over 5% and free cash flow guidance to approximately $14 billion.

## Financial Performance

### Q3 2025 Results (October 22, 2025)

**Total Revenue:** $16.3 billion, up 9% reported and 7% in constant currency
> "IBM reported Q3 2025 revenue of $16.3 billion, up 9% year-over-year and 7% in constant currency"

**Software Segment:**
- Revenue: $7.2 billion, up 10% reported, 9% constant currency
- Automation led with 22% growth
- Hybrid cloud (including Red Hat) increased 14%
- Data revenue rose 8%

> "Software revenue reached $7.2 billion, up 10% year-over-year (9% in constant currency). This represented one of the strongest performances across IBM's business segments."

Source: [CIO Dive - Infrastructure, software drive IBM Q3 growth](https://www.ciodive.com/news/IBM-earnings-Q3-2025-arvind-krishna/803676/)
