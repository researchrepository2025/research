# Google Cloud AI Platform - Product Announcements
## Date Range: August 1, 2025 - January 13, 2026

### Executive Summary
Between August 1, 2025 and January 13, 2026, Google Cloud launched a major expansion of its AI platform capabilities. Key developments include the general availability of Gemini 2.5 models (June/September), the launch of Gemini 3 model family (November/December), Gemini Enterprise platform (October), seventh-generation Ironwood TPUs (November), significant expansions to Vertex AI Agent Builder, and numerous updates to BigQuery AI, Model Garden, and generative media models (Imagen 4, Veo 3/3.1).

### Key Statistics
- Total announcements documented: 85+
- New products launched: 10+ (including Gemini Enterprise, Ironwood TPU, Gemini 3 family)
- Major updates: 50+ feature releases and general availability milestones
- Google Cloud Next 2025: 229 announcements (April 9-11)

### November 6, 2025 - Ironwood TPU (7th Generation) Announcement
**Event:** General Availability Announcement
**Category:** AI Infrastructure - TPU
**Type:** New Hardware Launch

**Description:**
Google announced on November 6, 2025 that the seventh-generation Tensor Processing Unit (TPU), called Ironwood, would be generally available in the coming weeks. Ironwood is the first Google TPU purpose-built for the age of inference, offering 10X peak performance improvement over TPU v5p and more than 4X better performance per chip for both training and inference workloads compared to TPU v6e (Trillium). Ironwood scales up to 9,216 liquid-cooled chips with breakthrough Inter-Chip Interconnect (ICI) networking spanning nearly 10 MW, delivering 42.5 Exaflops of compute power.

**Source Quote:**
> "Google announced on Thursday, November 6, 2025, that the seventh generation of its Tensor Processing Unit (TPU), called Ironwood, would hit the market for public use in the coming weeks"
> "Ironwood offers a 10X peak performance improvement over TPU v5p and more than 4X better performance per chip for both training and inference workloads compared to TPU v6e (Trillium)"
> "Scales up to 9,216 liquid cooled chips linked with breakthrough Inter-Chip Interconnect (ICI) networking spanning nearly 10 MW"
> "Delivers 42.5 Exaflops of compute power, making it more powerful than the world's largest supercomputer"

**Source:** [CNBC - Google unveils Ironwood](https://www.cnbc.com/2025/11/06/google-unveils-ironwood-seventh-generation-tpu-competing-with-nvidia.html) | Published: November 6, 2025
