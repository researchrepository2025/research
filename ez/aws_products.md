# AWS AI Platform - Product Announcements
## Date Range: August 1, 2025 - January 13, 2026

### Executive Summary
AWS AI/ML platform announcements during August 1, 2025 to January 13, 2026 centered predominantly around three major events: AWS Summit New York (July 16, 2025), AWS re:Invent 2025 (December 2-5, 2025), and continuous incremental releases throughout the period. The most significant development was the launch of Amazon Nova 2 model family with four new reasoning models, alongside major platform enhancements to Amazon Bedrock AgentCore for production AI agents, Amazon SageMaker serverless capabilities, and AWS's custom Trainium3 silicon. AWS expanded Amazon Bedrock to nearly 100 serverless models, introduced reinforcement fine-tuning delivering 66% accuracy gains, and launched AWS Transform for AI-powered code modernization. The period demonstrated AWS's strategic focus on agentic AI, model customization accessibility, and production-grade enterprise AI capabilities.

### Key Statistics
- Total announcements documented: 75+
- New products launched: 8 (Amazon Nova 2 family, Nova Forge, Nova Act, AWS Transform custom, Kiro autonomous agents, Amazon S3 Vectors GA, Bedrock AgentCore, Trainium3)
- Major updates to existing products: 40+
- Models added to Bedrock: 18 open weight models (bringing total to nearly 100)
